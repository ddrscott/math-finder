Math Finder

Bond with your kids while searching for number bonds! Play Number bonds teach kids the relationship between numbers.

What's Math Finder?

Math Finder is a fun app that helps kids learn addition bonds in an interactive or printable way. Just like solving a word search, you search for numbers that add up to make the equation true!

How Does it Work?

Math Finder uses a grid where you find numbers that add up make the addition bond! For instance, you search for 4 + 3 = 7, or any other addition bond. When you find it, circle it. Try to find them all!
